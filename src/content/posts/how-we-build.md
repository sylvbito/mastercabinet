## Our Process

Every Master Cabinet project follows a proven process that takes your project from initial conversation to finished installation.

### 1. Discovery

We start with a free video consultation over Zoom. You show us your space, share your ideas, and we discuss what's possible within your budget. This takes about 30 minutes.

### 2. Design

Our design team creates a detailed 3D plan of your new cabinetry, complete with material and finish selections. We refine it until every detail is right.

### 3. Quote

You receive a fixed-price quote covering design, materials, manufacturing, delivery, and installation. No hidden fees, no surprises.

### 4. Manufacturing

Your cabinetry is manufactured in our Melbourne workshop by experienced cabinet makers. We use precision CNC machinery for accurate cuts and hand-finishing for the details that matter.

### 5. Installation

Our installation team delivers and fits your cabinetry with minimal disruption to your home. We protect your floors, clean up daily, and make sure everything is perfect before we leave.

### 6. Sign-off

You inspect the finished work. We don't consider the job done until you're completely satisfied.
