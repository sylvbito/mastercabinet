Every piece of cabinetry we install starts in our workshop in Melbourne. We do not build on site — we measure, design, construct, finish, and assemble in a controlled environment before delivering and installing in a single coordinated effort.

**Why off-site construction matters:**

- **Consistent quality.** Temperature, humidity, and lighting are controlled. Cuts are precise. Finishes are applied in a dust-free environment.
- **No disruption to your home.** Your kitchen or laundry build happens in parallel with your renovation prep, not in sequence. No dust, no noise, no trades tripping over each other.
- **Faster installation.** Because everything is pre-assembled, our installers typically fit a full kitchen in 2–3 days.
- **Better joinery.** Our bench-mounted machinery produces tighter joints than anything we can bring to your site.

We take site measurements using laser distos and photographic reference, then model the space in CAD before a single sheet of material is cut. This eliminates the guesswork and ensures your cabinetry fits the first time.
